Our Story
About Veloxa

Built in Cape Town. Built for Africa.

We move Africa forward, one shipment at a time.

Founded in 2018 in Cape Town, Veloxa started as a two-truck operation running daily routes between Cape Town and Johannesburg. Today we operate a fleet of 80+ vehicles, 14 hubs, and serve over 400 businesses across Southern Africa.

Our mission is simple: make freight reliable, transparent, and affordable for every business — from the corner deli to the national retailer. We build technology that gives you real-time visibility and a team that actually answers the phone.
